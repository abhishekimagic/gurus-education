Gurus Philosophy

It is impossible to fear any trait if one takes professional training and practices it regularly in a fun conducive environment under the right guidance.

That is the basis of Guru's Education courses. Gurus team consists of very well-qualified and energetic professionals who are experts in their fields but all of them share a common passion for empowering the young generation. They provide the direction and leadership in assisting children to attain these life-changing skills: Building Confidence!”

Our Staff

Our staff is professional teachers with tremendous experience teaching Public Speaking, Debate, Philosophy, American Literature, and Theatre Arts. The team includes high school debate coaches, directors, artists, and authors. Our entire team is very passionate about teaching communication skills and debating, We believe that these skills are very important for an individual's success and that each student should master these skills.

Romeo Marquez Jr.

Romeo Marquez Jr. is an international keynote speaker, author of the #1 Amazon Best-selling book "The Success Messages," and creator & host of Superstar Success University. He has delivered more than a 1,000 presentations to over 200,000 audience members across the United States, Canada, Asia, and Europe on stages that range from TEDx to the National Speakers Association Convention.Romeo earned his B.A from UCLA's School of Theatre, Film & Television and had trained with an elite group of global success leaders personally invited by Jack Canfield, #1 New York Times Best-selling author and co-creator of the "Chicken Soup for the Soul" series. As a professor for theatre, film & television professor and acting coach, his clients have worked on television shows with major productions such as Disney, Nickelodeon, Cartoon Network, ABC, CBS, and various commercials. Romeo's greatest passion is empowering the younger generation to utilize their voice, talents, and gifts to create greater positive change in the world.

Ms. Jasmine Magallanes

Ms. Jasmine Magallanes graduated with highest honors in Communication Studies from Chabot College. She has mentored college level communication majors and developed Public Speaking and Debate programs. Jasmine is also a professional singer, actress, stage performer, 18 Time National and World Champion Martial Artist and Hall of Famer. Her acquired fame and skills have led to multiple Hollywood invitations and productions. Among being a VIP special guest and performing for the Bruce Lee “Enter the Dragon” reunion, she was also invited to walk the red carpet with Jackie Chan for the 2010 Hollywood premiere of “The Karate Kid” movie. Jasmine is an amazing teacher who utilizes her many talents and experiences to inspire and teach students the needed skills required to achieve their fullest potential.

Teirrah McNair

Teirrah McNair (aka Ms.T/Rev T) is an unstoppable people builder, pastor and published author on a mission to equip her audiences with well-crafted strategies for successful living. Whether on the road preaching or at home in the bay area facilitating soft skills workshops, Rev T brings a special blend of energy, humor and passion to the table. Her 25 plus years of experience includes seasons of coordinating an employment program for youth at risk, serving as an artist in residence for both California and Alaska’s art councils, as well as a nine-year stint as a literacy specialist. Additionally, she enjoyed a robust career as an actress and graced stages internationally with a wide array of quirky characters. One of those characters, Lucille Nadine Alexander, appears now as a character in one of the children’s books McNair has authored. McNair’s many published books can be found on Amazon at http://www.amazon.com/Teirrah-McNair/e/B0076ZX7W0
